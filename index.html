LOLY Glass

The LOLY Glass is a lottery reward system which promotes trading volume and incentivizes holders. As GLASS ($10,000) is filled, one lucky LOLY holder will be rewarded with an exclusive reward. Inactive LOLY traders for 7 days before the draw date will only be eligible.

LOLY STAKE

The $LOLY Ecosystem will utilise a staking system whereby users will be able to stake $LOLY to earn rewards. This will promote the number of holders while developers are able to focus on developing the protocol. Holders of staked LOLY tokens will be eligible to earn NFT or enter exclusive NFT presale.

BUSD Rewards

The ecosystem reflects BUSD through the anti-whale tax reward system. This gives investors the freedom to reinvest or to use as a type of transaction for other cryptocurrency payment integrated services. BUSD is a stable passive revenue coin, offering minimal volatility and maximum security regardless of market sentiment of being a stable coin.
